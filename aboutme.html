About me

Since forever, the realm of technology has held an irresistible fascination for me. The ceaseless curiosity for this tech world has always been a constant in my life.
For five years, my professional path led me to technical support, an experience rich in challenges, yet it didn't fully reveal my true passion: development. The idea of delving into codes and crafting digital solutions lay dormant but on the verge of awakening.
Today, twelve years after embarking on this professional journey, I am thrilled to have the opportunity to commence my true venture into the field of technology. This is the moment to turn my curiosity into tangible knowledge, to absorb every line of code as part of a unique language.
I'm eager to soak up everything this journey has to offer, to learn, grow, and, above all, to apply these newfound skills in practice. May this phase be filled with discoveries, challenges overcome, and achievements.
